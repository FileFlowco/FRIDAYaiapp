Privacy by design

Built from the ground up
to never leak data.

Most AI assistants are cloud services wearing an app costume. FRIDAY is genuinely different — the AI never leaves your phone.

  • All inference runs locally — your words are never transmitted to any server
  • No account required, no email, no tracking ID
  • Conversations stored only in your device's encrypted local storage
  • Optional cloud providers (OpenAI / Claude) only activate when you explicitly enable them
  • No ads, no analytics, no third-party SDKs that phone home
  • Works completely in Airplane Mode once the model is downloaded

Common questions

Quick answers

Why isn't the model downloading?

Make sure you're on Wi-Fi and the app is in the foreground. The download can take 5–15 minutes depending on your connection. If it fails, tap Retry in the download screen.

Does FRIDAY work without internet?

Yes — completely. Once the model is downloaded, FRIDAY works in Airplane Mode. No internet required for any core functionality unless you enable a cloud provider.

My API key isn't working. Help?

Make sure you're pasting the full key including any prefix (e.g. "sk-..."). Check that your account has API access enabled and that your billing is set up on the provider's dashboard.

Is FRIDAY free?

Yes. FRIDAY is free to download and use indefinitely. On-device AI has no cost. If you use a cloud provider, you pay that provider directly — we take nothing.

Can I delete my data?

Your data is only on your device. To delete it, clear FRIDAY's data in iOS Settings → FRIDAY, or delete the app. We have no copy of anything.

What iPhones are supported?

iPhone 12 or newer running iOS 17 or later. The 3B model (default) works best on iPhone 14 and newer. See the Models page for details.
